Test case luong PR duyet tao yeu cau cap phat

Muc tieu
1. Khi giam doc duyet xong PR, he thong tao yeu cau cap phat.
2. Yeu cau cap phat cho nhan hang xong moi chuyen cho ke toan.
3. Ke toan chi duyet khi da nhan du hang.

Nhom 1. Tao yeu cau cap phat sau khi duyet PR

TC01
Ten case: Tao yeu cau cap phat tu dong
Dieu kien: PR hop le, dang cho giam doc duyet
Buoc:
1. Truong phong tao PR
2. Giam doc duyet buoc cuoi
Ket qua mong doi:
1. Tao duoc 1 yeu cau cap phat
2. Danh sach hang trong yeu cau cap phat lay tu PR
3. Trang thai la cho nhan hang

TC02
Ten case: PR khong co du lieu hop le
Dieu kien: PR khong co dong hang hop le
Buoc:
1. Giam doc duyet PR
Ket qua mong doi:
1. Khong tao yeu cau cap phat

TC03
Ten case: Khong tao trung
Dieu kien: PR da co yeu cau cap phat roi
Buoc:
1. Kiem tra lai sau thao tac duyet
Ket qua mong doi:
1. Moi PR chi co 1 yeu cau cap phat
